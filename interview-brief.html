Executive brief

Parallel growth with reliability.

A candidate hypothesis for managing the pressure between hyperscale expansion and the operating disciplines that protect people, plant, customers and capital.

Actual mandate: make a growing Southwest campus portfolio safer, more reliable, more financially legible and more customer-trusted while integrating new capacity, vendors, technology and operating standards into 24/7 execution.

Company moment

$3.0B in North American development financing; 500MW+ Houston potential; CTX2 moving into MEP and commissioning; high-density, water-conscious infrastructure; a role-level 4x growth ambition.

Central contradiction

Speed-to-market creates value only when the live campus can absorb change without borrowing reliability from tomorrow.

White-space thesis

Use a Campus Paralleling Review to make authority, plant condition, procedure, partner readiness and customer continuity explicit before work proceeds.

Strongest objection

No claimed direct colocation tenure or unverified Tier/CFM/CEM/PE credentials. The argument is adjacent high-consequence operating evidence plus respect for experienced technical authority.

Operating system

The Campus Paralleling Review

A one-line decision model: the live campus and growth/change path connect only when five interlocks carry evidence and ownership.

1. Authority

Life-safety, technical, operational and customer decision rights; escalation and stop-work boundaries.

2. Plant condition

Current redundancy, alarm, maintenance and dependency state sufficient for the proposed action.

3. Procedure

Approved method, contingency, stop conditions, evidence requirements and closure definition.

4. Partner readiness

Internal and vendor owners, parts, schedule, competence and accountability are confirmed.

5. Customer continuity

Impact, communication, SLA implications, escalation and trust consequences remain visible.

Learning closure

Results, recurrence, new standard work and portfolio-level reuse are captured after execution.

Decision states

StateMeaningLeadership action
Proceed with controlsRequired interlocks are closed with explicit owners and evidence.Execute, monitor and close learning.
Bound and replanWork is viable, but one or more dependencies need constraint.Reduce scope, change window or strengthen contingency.
EscalateRisk or customer consequence exceeds local decision boundary.Bring the right authority and options, not merely the problem.
Hold / stopPlant state, procedure, authority or evidence is insufficient.Protect the live campus and remove pressure to normalize uncertainty.
AI's bounded role: help synthesize history, recurring alarms, work-order text, vendor patterns and evidence gaps. Human technical and life-safety authority retains the decision.
