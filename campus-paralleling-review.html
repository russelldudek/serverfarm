Balanced evidence review

People

Safety, workload, qualification, authority confidence and learning.

Plant

Availability, recurrence, work aging, redundancy and asset condition.

Customer

Continuity, communication, escalation and commitment trust.

Economics

Vendor performance, OPEX/CAPEX implications, rework and lifecycle value.

AI-assisted, human-owned: synthesis may help find patterns across alarms, work orders and records. Technical, safety and customer decisions remain accountable to people with the relevant authority.
